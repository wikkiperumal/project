OOTY

Ooty, also known as Udhagamandalam, is a popular hill station located in the Nilgiri Hills of the Indian state of Tamil Nadu. It is situated at an elevation of approximately 2,240 meters (7,350 feet) above sea level, making it a favored destination for tourists seeking cooler temperatures and scenic beauty.

YERCAUD

Yercaud is a hill station located in the Shevaroy Hills of the Eastern Ghats in Tamil Nadu, India. It is situated at an altitude of approximately 1,500 meters (4,920 feet) above sea level and offers a pleasant climate throughout the year. Yercaud is known for its scenic beauty, lush greenery, and serene atmosphere.

KOLLI HILLS

Kolli Hills is a mountain range located in the Namakkal district of Tamil Nadu, India. It is a lesser-known hill station but is gaining popularity among nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts. Kolli Hills is known for its untouched natural beauty, scenic landscapes, and the famous Agaya Gangai Waterfalls.
